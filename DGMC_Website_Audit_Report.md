## Executive Summary

DGMC AI Consultancy is a Cambridge-based AI consultancy targeting small businesses. The website is built on Wix and presents a clean visual design. However, a detailed audit reveals significant gaps between the brand promise (cutting-edge AI automation, chatbot creation) and the actual content delivered to visitors. The site suffers from incomplete page builds (placeholder URLs, empty project pages), critical spelling errors, missing contact information, and a lack of social proof — all of which are actively suppressing enquiries and conversion rates.

This report provides a full audit of every page on the site and delivers actionable improvements across four strategic areas: conversion rate optimisation, SEO, technical health, and service prioritisation.

## Part 2 — Five Improvements to Increase Conversions & Enquiries

### Improvement 1: Create Dedicated, Outcome-Focused Landing Pages for Each Core Service

**The Problem:**
The homepage mentions four distinct services — AI Automation, Chatbot Creation, Web Development, and Video Marketing — but these services have no dedicated pages. Prospects interested in a specific service hit a dead end.

**The Fix:**
Build individual landing pages for each service with:
- A clear headline targeting the prospect's pain point (e.g. 'Cut Customer Support Costs by 60% with a Custom AI Chatbot')
- 3-step process breakdown: Discovery > Build > Deploy
- Outcome-based bullet points (not feature lists)
- A form or CTA to book a free demo/discovery call
- Relevant testimonial or mini case study

**Expected Impact:**
Dedicated service pages dramatically increase conversion rates by matching visitor intent and removing decision fatigue.

---

### Improvement 2: Add Prominent Social Proof (Testimonials, Results, Client Logos)

**The Problem:**
There is zero social proof on the entire website. No testimonials, no client names, no measurable outcomes, no case studies, no before/after results.

**The Fix:**
- Add 3-5 client testimonials to the homepage (real quotes with photo, name, and business name)
- Create a 'Results' section showing measurable outcomes: 'Helped [Client X] save 15 hours/week using AI workflow automation'
- If no clients yet: record a video demo of the chatbot/automation product in action and embed as a product showcase
- Add trust badges or partnership logos (Google Partner, Microsoft for Startups, Meta Business Partner if applicable)

**Expected Impact:**
Social proof is the single highest-converting element for B2B services. Businesses choose consultants they trust.

---

### Improvement 3: Add Real Business Contact Information Everywhere

**The Problem:**
The contact page has no email, no phone number, and no address. Serious B2B buyers will immediately exit when they cannot verify the business is real.

**The Fix:**
- Add a professional email address (e.g. hello@dgmc-ai-consultancy.com) to the header, footer, and contact page
- Add a UK phone number (or WhatsApp Business number) in the header
- Add 'Cambridge, United Kingdom' in the footer
- Add a Google Maps embed on the contact page

**Expected Impact:**
Contact information is a fundamental trust signal. Its absence is a conversion killer, especially for a B2B AI consultancy where contracts may be £1,000-£10,000+.

---

### Improvement 4: Implement a High-Value Lead Magnet Above the Fold

**The Problem:**
The homepage has a newsletter subscribe form with no reason to sign up. Visitors have no compelling reason to give their email address.

**The Fix:**
- Create a free downloadable resource: 'The Small Business AI Toolkit: 10 Automations That Save 20+ Hours Per Month'
- Gate it behind a simple email capture form
- Add this offer as a prominent section below the hero heading
- Set up an automated email sequence to nurture leads post-download

**Expected Impact:**
Lead magnets typically convert at 20-40% on relevant traffic vs. 1-3% for generic newsletter signups. This builds an email list of qualified prospects.

---

### Improvement 5: Redesign the Hero Section with a Single, Compelling Value Proposition

**The Problem:**
The current H1 is 'Welcome to DGMC AI Consultancy' — a generic placeholder that communicates nothing about what the business does or why a visitor should care. There is no immediate CTA above the fold.

**The Fix:**
- New hero headline: 'AI Automation & Chatbots That Save Small Businesses 20+ Hours a Week'
- New subheadline: 'DGMC helps small businesses in the UK implement smart AI tools that cut costs, grow sales, and run on autopilot.'
- Primary CTA button: 'Book a Free AI Strategy Session'
- Secondary CTA: 'See How It Works'
- Trust line: 'Trusted by small businesses across Cambridge & the UK'

**Expected Impact:**
A clear value proposition + CTA above the fold is the most foundational CRO change possible. Studies consistently show this single change can increase conversions by 20-100%.

## Part 3 — Five SEO Improvements

### SEO Improvement 1: Fix All URL Slugs Immediately

**The Problem:**
/blank-3 (Services), /blank-5 (Projects), /blank-6 (Contact) are default Wix template slugs. Search engines index these meaningless paths and they carry no keyword relevance. They also make the site appear unfinished to both humans and crawlers.

**The Fix:**
- Rename /blank-3 to /services
- Rename /blank-5 to /portfolio or /case-studies
- Rename /blank-6 to /contact
- Set up 301 redirects from all old slugs to new ones

**Why This Matters:**
Clean, descriptive URLs are a direct ranking factor and increase click-through rates in SERPs by ~10-15% due to higher relevance signals.

### SEO Improvement 4: Build an E-E-A-T-Compliant Blog & Internal Linking Strategy

**The Problem:**
The two blog articles are attributed to 'mcw2k18' (a user ID), not a named professional. Google's E-E-A-T guidelines (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trustworthiness) heavily penalise anonymous or pseudonymous authorship in professional/technical niches.

**The Fix:**
- Assign blog posts to a named author with a bio, headshot, LinkedIn link, and professional credentials
- Create an 'About the Author' section at the end of each post
- Publish a minimum of 2-4 targeted articles per month on topics like:
  - 'Best AI Chatbot Tools for UK Small Businesses in 2026'
  - 'How to Automate Customer Onboarding with AI'
  - 'AI Marketing vs Traditional Marketing: A Cost Comparison for UK SMEs'
- Add internal links from every blog post to the relevant service page

**Why This Matters:**
E-E-A-T is a critical ranking signal in Google's Search Quality Rater Guidelines. Named, credentialed authors dramatically increase organic rankings for competitive B2B keywords.
